Welcome to the Vagabond Onboarding Tool

  • To Begin, Login with your User account and Password.

  • Select the location you would like to edit from the list

  • Enter information about your location in the form

  • Provide a clear photo of the face of the machine

  • Press submit and await the green confirmation notice, displayed on the page.

  • Select a new location from the list, or close the browser window, you will automatically be logged out on leaving the page.
